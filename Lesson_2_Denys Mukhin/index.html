Covid-19: Pfizer/BioNTech vaccine approved for use next week in UK

Britain's medicines regulator, the MHRA, says the jab, which offers up to 95% protection against Covid-19 illness, is safe to be rolled out.The first 800,000 doses will be available in the UK from next week, Health Secretary Matt Hancock said.
